Biography


I began my life in Pessac then moved to the Bassin d'Arcachon. My mother is half Vietnamese native of Tahiti and my dad limogeois, less exotic! I am the product of this coktail. With a mom stewardess, I had the opportunity to travel and become open-minded with a vision of the world, our country and myself. I always was a small handywoman and a resourceful person. I really discovered the art at the high school with a golden professor, who allowed me to develop me. So, I chose the ECV to study, where I am now, but I try begin to make some projects live which mean a lot to me.
